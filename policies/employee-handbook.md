# Employee Handbook

## Welcome to TechCorp

Welcome to TechCorp! This handbook contains important information about our company policies, procedures, and benefits. Please read it carefully and keep it for reference.

## Company Mission

TechCorp is committed to delivering innovative technology solutions while maintaining a positive, inclusive work environment that empowers our employees to grow and succeed.

## Core Values

- **Innovation**: We embrace new ideas and creative solutions
- **Integrity**: We act with honesty and transparency in all our dealings
- **Collaboration**: We work together to achieve common goals
- **Excellence**: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do
- **Respect**: We treat everyone with dignity and respect

## Employment Policies

### Equal Opportunity Employment

TechCorp is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

### At-Will Employment

Employment with TechCorp is at-will, meaning either the employee or the company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or notice.

### Probationary Period

New employees serve a 90-day probationary period during which performance and fit are evaluated. During this time, benefits may be limited.

## Work Schedule and Attendance

### Standard Work Hours

Our standard work week is Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, with a one-hour lunch break. Total work week is 40 hours.

### Flexible Work Arrangements

TechCorp supports flexible work arrangements including:
- Flexible start/end times (core hours 10 AM - 3 PM required)
- Remote work options (up to 3 days per week with manager approval)
- Compressed work weeks (subject to business needs)

### Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is essential. Employees must notify their supervisor as early as possible if they will be late or absent. Excessive absenteeism may result in disciplinary action.

## Professional Conduct

### Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues, customers, and vendors with respect
- Maintain confidentiality of company and customer information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Report any unethical behavior or policy violations

### Dress Code

TechCorp maintains a business casual dress code. Clothing should be professional, clean, and appropriate for the workplace. On Fridays, casual dress is permitted.

### Communication Standards

- Use professional language in all business communications
- Respond to emails and messages within 24 hours during business days
- Keep meetings focused and productive
- Respect others' time and schedules
