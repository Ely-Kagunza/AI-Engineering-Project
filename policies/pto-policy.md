# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

## Overview

TechCorp provides paid time off to support work-life balance and employee well-being. This policy covers vacation time, sick leave, and personal days.

## PTO Accrual

### Vacation Time

PTO accrues based on length of service:

- **0-2 years**: 15 days per year (1.25 days per month)
- **3-5 years**: 20 days per year (1.67 days per month)
- **6+ years**: 25 days per year (2.08 days per month)

### Sick Leave

All employees accrue sick leave at a rate of 8 hours per month, up to a maximum of 96 hours (12 days) per year.

### Personal Days

All employees receive 3 personal days per calendar year, available on January 1st.

## PTO Usage Guidelines

### Advance Notice

- **1-3 days**: 48 hours advance notice
- **4-10 days**: 2 weeks advance notice  
- **10+ days**: 1 month advance notice

### Approval Process

1. Submit PTO request through HR system
2. Manager reviews and approves/denies within 3 business days
3. HR confirms approval and updates records

### Blackout Periods

PTO requests may be restricted during:
- End of fiscal year (December 15-31)
- Major product launches
- Peak business periods (as designated by management)

## Sick Leave Specifics

### Eligible Uses

Sick leave may be used for:
- Personal illness or injury
- Medical appointments
- Caring for immediate family members
- Mental health days

### Documentation

- **1-2 days**: No documentation required
- **3+ consecutive days**: Medical certification may be required
- **Frequent absences**: Pattern documentation may be requested

## Holiday Schedule

### Observed Holidays

TechCorp observes the following paid holidays:

- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Day after Thanksgiving
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve

### Floating Holidays

Employees receive 2 floating holidays per year to observe personal or religious holidays not listed above.

## PTO Payout and Carryover

### Year-End Carryover

- Maximum 40 hours of vacation time may carry over to the next year
- Sick leave carries over with no maximum
- Personal days do not carry over

### Termination Payout

Upon termination, employees will be paid for:
- Unused vacation time (up to maximum accrual limits)
- Unused personal days
- Sick leave is not paid out upon termination

## Special Leave

### Bereavement Leave

Up to 5 days paid leave for immediate family members (spouse, children, parents, siblings).
Up to 3 days paid leave for extended family members.

### Jury Duty

Employees receive full pay while serving jury duty. Any jury pay received should be remitted to the company.

### Military Leave

TechCorp complies with all federal and state military leave requirements.
