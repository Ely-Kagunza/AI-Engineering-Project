# Information Security Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes security standards to protect TechCorp's information assets, systems, and data from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, vendors, and third parties who have access to TechCorp systems, networks, or data.

## Information Classification

### Data Categories

**Public Information**
- Marketing materials and public website content
- Published research and whitepapers
- General company information
- No special handling required

**Internal Information**
- Employee directories and organizational charts
- Internal policies and procedures
- Project plans and status reports
- Requires basic access controls

**Confidential Information**
- Customer data and contact information
- Financial records and business plans
- Proprietary software and algorithms
- Requires encryption and access logging

**Restricted Information**
- Personal employee information (SSN, salary, medical)
- Customer payment information
- Trade secrets and intellectual property
- Requires highest level of protection

## Access Control

### User Account Management

**Account Creation**
- Accounts created only upon manager approval
- Unique usernames for each individual
- Strong password requirements enforced
- Multi-factor authentication required for all systems

**Access Permissions**
- Principle of least privilege applied
- Role-based access control implemented
- Regular access reviews conducted quarterly
- Immediate revocation upon termination

### Password Requirements

**Complexity Standards**
- Minimum 12 characters in length
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Cannot contain dictionary words or personal information
- Cannot reuse last 12 passwords

**Management Practices**
- Change passwords every 90 days
- Use password manager for unique passwords
- Never share passwords with others
- Report compromised passwords immediately

## Network Security

### Wireless Networks

**Corporate Wi-Fi**
- WPA3 encryption required
- Guest network separated from corporate network
- Regular security key rotation
- Device registration required

**Personal Devices**
- BYOD policy requires security compliance
- Mobile device management (MDM) enrollment
- Remote wipe capability enabled
- Personal and business data separation

### Remote Access

**VPN Requirements**
- VPN required for all remote connections
- Multi-factor authentication for VPN access
- Split tunneling prohibited
- Connection logging and monitoring

**Home Networks**
- Secure Wi-Fi with WPA3 encryption
- Regular router firmware updates
- No public Wi-Fi for business activities
- Firewall enabled on all devices

## Data Protection

### Data Handling

**Storage Requirements**
- Confidential data encrypted at rest
- Cloud storage must be approved services only
- Local storage on encrypted devices only
- Regular backup and recovery testing

**Transmission Security**
- Email encryption for confidential information
- Secure file transfer protocols (SFTP, HTTPS)
- No confidential data via personal email
- Encrypted messaging for sensitive communications

### Data Retention

**Retention Periods**
- Employee records: 7 years after termination
- Financial records: 7 years after fiscal year end
- Customer data: Per contractual agreements
- Email: 3 years for business communications

**Secure Disposal**
- Physical media destruction for sensitive data
- Secure deletion software for electronic data
- Certificate of destruction for compliance
- Asset disposal tracking and documentation

## Incident Response

### Security Incidents

**Reporting Requirements**
- Report suspected incidents immediately
- Contact IT security team within 1 hour
- Document all relevant details
- Preserve evidence when possible

**Response Procedures**
1. Immediate containment of threat
2. Assessment of impact and scope
3. Notification of affected parties
4. Recovery and restoration of services
5. Post-incident review and improvements

### Breach Notification

**Internal Notification**
- IT Security team immediately
- Management within 2 hours
- Legal and compliance teams as needed
- HR for employee-related incidents

**External Notification**
- Customers within 24 hours if data affected
- Regulatory authorities per legal requirements
- Law enforcement if criminal activity suspected
- Insurance carriers for coverage claims

## Physical Security

### Facility Access

**Access Controls**
- Badge access required for all areas
- Visitor escort policy enforced
- Security cameras in common areas
- After-hours access logging

**Clean Desk Policy**
- Lock screens when away from desk
- Secure confidential documents when not in use
- No sensitive information on whiteboards
- Shred confidential documents before disposal

### Equipment Security

**Device Management**
- Asset inventory and tracking
- Encryption required on all devices
- Automatic screen locks after 10 minutes
- Remote wipe capability for lost devices

**Theft Prevention**
- Cable locks for desktop computers
- Secure storage for portable devices
- Equipment check-out procedures
- Insurance coverage for business equipment

## Compliance and Training

### Security Awareness

**Training Requirements**
- Annual security awareness training for all employees
- Phishing simulation exercises quarterly
- New employee security orientation
- Role-specific security training as needed

**Communication**
- Monthly security tips and updates
- Incident alerts and lessons learned
- Policy updates and reminders
- Security metrics and performance reports

### Compliance Monitoring

**Regular Assessments**
- Quarterly vulnerability scans
- Annual penetration testing
- Security audit and compliance reviews
- Third-party security assessments

**Metrics and Reporting**
- Security incident tracking and trends
- Compliance status reporting
- Training completion rates
- Risk assessment updates

## Third-Party Security

### Vendor Management

**Due Diligence**
- Security questionnaires for all vendors
- Contractual security requirements
- Regular security reviews
- Incident notification requirements

**Data Sharing**
- Data processing agreements required
- Minimum necessary data sharing
- Secure transmission methods
- Regular access reviews and updates

## Policy Violations

### Consequences

**Minor Violations**
- Verbal warning and retraining
- Written warning for repeat offenses
- Temporary access restrictions
- Additional monitoring requirements

**Major Violations**
- Immediate access suspension
- Formal disciplinary action
- Termination for serious breaches
- Legal action if warranted

### Reporting Violations

- Report violations to IT Security or HR
- Anonymous reporting options available
- No retaliation for good faith reporting
- Investigation and resolution tracking
